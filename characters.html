Simba

Simba is the heart of The Lion King. He’s a curious and spirited lion cub destined to become king. His journey from playful innocence to courageous leadership is shaped by love, loss, and the wisdom of those around him.

Nala

Nala is a fierce and loyal lioness whose strength lies in both her courage and compassion. As Simba’s childhood friend and future queen, she plays a pivotal role in urging him to confront his past and reclaim his destiny.

Mufasa

Mufasa is the noble and wise king of the Pride Lands. As Simba’s father, he instills the values of responsibility and respect for the Circle of Life. Even after his death, Mufasa’s spirit continues to guide Simba as he becomes the next king.

Scar

Scar is Mufasa’s envious brother, whose thirst for power drives him to betray his family and plunge the Pride Lands into darkness. He is a selfish and greedy leader who rules through fear and deception.

Timon

Timon is a fast-talking meerkat with a sharp wit and a flair for drama. Alongside his best friend Pumbaa, he introduces Simba to a carefree lifestyle under the motto “Hakuna Matata.”

Pumbaa

Pumbaa is a lovable warthog with a gentle heart and a quirky sense of humor. Alongside Timon, Pumbaa helps Simba rediscover joy and freedom, proving that kindness and courage come in all shapes and sizes.

Zazu

Zazu is a hornbill bird who serves as Mufasa’s royal advisor. Despite his fussiness, Zazu is deeply committed to the kingdom and plays a key role in supporting Simba’s return to power.

Rafiki

Rafiki is the enlightened mandrill who serves as the spiritual guide of the Pride Lands. With wisdom and playful unpredictability, he helps Simba reconnect with his true self.
