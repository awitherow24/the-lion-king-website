The Cast

The Lion King features a star-studded cast that brings the beloved characters to life with their memorable performances. Matthew Broderick voices the adult Simba, capturing the character's journey from a carefree cub to a responsible king. James Earl Jones delivers a powerful performance as Mufasa, Simba's wise and noble father. Jeremy Irons portrays the cunning and villainous Scar, adding depth to the character's malevolence. Nathan Lane and Ernie Sabella provide comic relief as Timon and Pumbaa, the lovable meerkat and warthog duo. The film also features the voices of Moira Kelly as Nala, Simba's childhood friend and eventual queen, and Rowan Atkinson as Zazu, the uptight hornbill advisor. The ensemble cast's dynamic performances contribute significantly to the film's enduring appeal and emotional resonance.
